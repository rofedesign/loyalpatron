MERCHANT INQUIRIES AND SUPPORT

Loyal Patron is dedicated to providing our valued merchants with prompt and helpful support over all technical, billing and account questions.

Prospective Clients

If interested in becoming a participating merchant and offering Loyal Patron services in your business, please complete our Merchant Inquiry Form. You may also reach us directly at 800-978-1338 x1 for Sales.

Existing Clients:


Non-urgent questions/issues? Please complete a support ticket, which are usually resolved within 1 business day, often within minutes.

Urgent questions/issues? You can call us Toll Free: 1.800.978.1338 x2 or create a support ticket. Support Hours: Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM Mountain Standard Time (closed weekends and major holidays)
