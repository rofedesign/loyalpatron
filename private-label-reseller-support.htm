Private Label and Reseller Support

Loyal Patron is dedicated to providing our authorized distributors with the tools and information needed to successfully manage their merchant portfolio.

Our prompt and courteous support is available to help you with technical, billing and account questions.

Prospective Distributors:

If interested in becoming a participating Reseller or Private Label Partner, please complete our Reseller Inquiry Form.

Existing Resellers and Private Label Partners:

Non-urgent questions/issues? Please complete a support ticket, which are usually resolved within 1 business day, often within minutes.

Urgent questions/issues? You can call us Toll Free: 1.800.978.1338 x2 or create a support ticket. Support Hours: Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM Mountain Standard Time (closed weekends and major holidays)
